## üöÄ Getting Started

## üìù Creating Your Account

### Registration Process

1. **Click "Get Started Free"** on the homepage

### First-Time Setup

After successful registration:

## üé≠ Analyzing Your Emotions

### Step 1: Navigate to Emotion Capture

1. **Click "Capture"** in the main navigation menu
2. **You'll see the emotion analysis page** with camera interface

### Step 2: Choose Your Method

#### Option A: Live Camera Capture (Recommended)

1. **Allow camera access** when prompted by your browser
2. **Position yourself** in good lighting
3. **Look directly at the camera** with a natural expression
4. **Click the "Capture Photo" button** when ready
5. **Wait 2-3 seconds** for AI analysis

#### Option B: Upload Photo

1. **Click "Upload Photo"** tab
2. **Choose a photo** from your device

### Automatic Recommendations

### Understanding Recommendations

#### Track Information

#### Why These Songs?

The AI considers:
- **Your detected emotion** (primary factor)

## üîß Troubleshooting

### Common Issues and Solutions

#### Camera Not Working

**Symptoms**: Camera doesn't load or shows error

**Solutions**:
1. **Check browser permissions**:
   - Look for camera icon in address bar
   - Click and select "Allow"
2. **Try different browser** (Chrome recommended)
3. **Check if camera is in use** by other applications
4. **Refresh the page** and try again
5. **Use photo upload** as alternative

#### No Music Recommendations

**Symptoms**: Emotion detected but no music appears

**Solutions**:
1. **Check internet connection**
2. **Refresh the page** and try again
3. **Try different emotion** or retake photo
4. **Check if Spotify is accessible** in your region
5. **Wait a moment** - sometimes loading takes time

#### Poor Emotion Detection

**Symptoms**: Incorrect or low confidence results

**Solutions**:
1. **Improve lighting**:
   - Face bright, natural light
   - Avoid backlighting
   - Use lamp if needed
2. **Position correctly**:
   - Look directly at camera
   - Keep face centered
   - Maintain normal distance
3. **Clear expression**:
   - Express your actual emotion
   - Don't force expressions
   - Try multiple captures

#### Slow Performance

**Symptoms**: App loads slowly or freezes

**Solutions**:
1. **Close other browser tabs**
2. **Clear browser cache** and reload
3. **Check internet speed**
4. **Try different browser**
5. **Restart browser** completely

#### Data Not Saving

**Symptoms**: History or preferences not persisting

**Solutions**:
1. **Check if logged in** properly
2. **Allow browser storage** permissions
3. **Don't use incognito/private mode**
4. **Clear cache** and log in again
5. **Check browser storage settings**

## üîí Privacy and Security

### Your Data Privacy

#### What We Collect
- **Emotion analysis results** (for recommendations)
- **Music preferences** (for better suggestions)
- **Usage statistics** (anonymous analytics)
- **Account information** (name, email)

#### What We DON'T Collect
- **Facial images** (processed locally, not stored)
- **Personal conversations** or messages
- **Financial information**
- **Unnecessary personal data**

#### Data Storage
- **Emotions and preferences**: Stored locally in your browser
- **Account info**: Secure servers with encryption
- **Facial analysis**: Processed in browser, not transmitted
- **Music data**: Retrieved from Spotify, not stored

#### Your Rights
- **View all your data** in History section
- **Export your data** anytime
- **Delete your data** instantly
- **Control data collection** in Settings

### Security Measures

#### Account Security
- **Secure authentication** with NextAuth.js
- **OAuth integration** with Spotify
- **Session management** with automatic timeout
- **Password protection** (if using email signup)

#### Data Protection
- **HTTPS encryption** for all communications
- **No sensitive data storage** in browser
- **Regular security updates**
- **Privacy-first design**
